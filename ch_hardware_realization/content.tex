\chapter[Hardware Realization of the Time-Variant DPS]{Hardware Realization of the Time-Variant \acs{DPS}}
\label{ch:hardware-realization}


Designing the proposed \acs{DPS} involves combining the precise control of
digital technology with the reliable performance of analog circuits.
This chapter will walk through the process of realizing the concept into
a working prototype.

We will look at how to take the concept into a working prototype and cover the
challenges and solutions that make the design work in real-world conditions.
This chapter will be an overview of the important decisions involved in
realizing a working system according to the proposed system architecture
presented in \autoref{ch:proposed-control-loop} \autoref{sec:system-overview}.
