\section{Outline of this work}
\label{sec:work-outline}

In \autoref{sec:design-and-test-challenges} it was stated that safety
and reliability are design tasks.

One of the most significant tools to evaluate the safety,
reliability, and functionality of electronic components and systems,
is an \ac{ATE} or simply a \emph{tester}.
An \ac{ATE} provides power, test signals, and test conditions known as
stimuli to a \ac{DUT}.

Throughout the rest of this work, at first, a running \ac{ATE} environment will be
evaluated for finding bottlenecks that affect yield.
Then, based on the findings an \ac{ATE}-grade power supply system will be
designed and implemented to demonstrate the capabilities of a safe and
reliable \ac{DPS} based on research work that is presented here.
